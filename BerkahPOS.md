# Privacy Policy for BerkahPOS

**Last updated:** December 2, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how **Hen** ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your information in connection with your use of our application, **BerkahPOS** (the "App").

## 1. Information Collection and Use

BerkahPOS is a paid application intended for use as is. We do not collect personally identifiable information for marketing or advertising purposes.

The App requires specific permissions to function correctly as a Point of Sales system. The data accessed through these permissions is stored locally on your device and is not uploaded to our servers unless you explicitly initiate a backup or export.

### A. Device Permissions
The App requests the following permissions:

* **Camera:** This permission is used exclusively to scan barcodes or QR codes for products during transactions.
* **Storage (Read/Write External Storage):** This permission is required to:
    * Save transaction reports (PDF/Excel) to your device.
    * Backup and restore your database.
    * Select product images from your gallery.

### B. Personal Data
The App uses a username and password system for authentication. This information is stored locally on your device to secure access to the App. We do not have access to your password.

## 2. Third-Party Services

We **do not** use third-party advertising services (like AdMob) or third-party analytics services (like Firebase Analytics). We prioritize your privacy and do not track your usage behavior.

## 3. Data Retention and Deletion

Since BerkahPOS stores data locally on your device:
* **Retention:** Your transaction data, product lists, and settings remain on your device as long as the App is installed.
* **Deletion:** You can delete all your data simply by uninstalling the App or clearing the App data via your Android settings.
* **Request:** If you have questions about data handling, you can contact us via the link provided at the end of this policy.

## 4. Security

We value your trust in providing us your Personal Information. Since the data is stored locally, the security of your data also depends on the security of your device. We strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your database integrity.

## 5. Children's Privacy

These Services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

## 6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
